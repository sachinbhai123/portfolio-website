Extra Curricular Activities

Nepali Zaika - Food Stall

Nepali Zaika was a food stall event where we served a variety of traditional Nepali dishes, showcasing the diverse flavors of Nepalese cuisine. The stall featured chatpate, sel roti (sweet rice bread), gundruk chips (noble product), Gilo-chatpate.

Our main goal was to promote Nepali culture through food and let people experience authentic flavors. We also managed customer service, handled transactions, and improved teamwork skills while working in a fast-paced environment.

Badminton

Playing badminton has been a great way for me to stay active and improve my reflexes. I have participated in local tournaments and college competitions, enhancing my hand-eye coordination and endurance.

This sport has also taught me patience, strategy, and quick decision-making under pressure. I enjoy both singles and doubles matches, working on teamwork and agility.

Cricket

Cricket has always been one of my favorite sports. As a player, I have taken part in inter-college and friendly matches, primarily playing as a **batsman and occasional bowler**.

The game has helped me develop leadership, teamwork, and perseverance. The strategic aspect of cricket, along with the requirement for coordination and communication, has given me valuable experiences beyond just physical fitness.
